--- RECIPE 1 ---
Grandma's Chicken Biryani

OK so for this you need like 500g of basmati rice (long grain), about 750 grams chicken (cut into pieces),
2 onions sliced thin, yogurt maybe 1 cup, 4 cloves of garlic and a small piece of ginger.
Spices: cumin seeds, cardamom 3-4 pods, bay leaves 2, cinnamon 1 stick, biryani masala 2 tbsp,
salt to taste, saffron soaked in warm milk, fresh coriander leaves for garnishing.

Cook time is usually around 45 mins if you know what you're doing. Serves 4 people.
Suitable for non-vegetarians obviously. Medium difficulty I'd say.

Steps are: First wash and soak rice for 30 mins. Marinate chicken in yogurt+spices for 1 hour.
Fry the onions golden brown in ghee. Layer rice and chicken in a heavy pot.
Seal with dough and cook on dum for 25 minutes on low flame. Serve hot.

--- RECIPE 2 ---
Quick Pasta Aglio e Olio (serves 2)

All you need: 200g spaghetti, 6 garlic cloves (sliced), red chili flakes (1 tsp or more if you like heat),
good olive oil (4 tbsp), handful of fresh parsley, parmesan cheese for topping, salt and pepper.
It's vegan if you skip the cheese! Takes about 20 minutes. Easy level.

How to make it: Boil pasta in very salty water. While that's going, slowly cook garlic in olive oil
till golden (dont burn it!!). Add chili flakes. Drain pasta keeping some pasta water.
Toss everything together adding pasta water as needed for silkiness. Top with parsley and parmesan.

--- RECIPE 3 ---
Mango Lassi (4 glasses)

Ingredients needed: 2 ripe mangoes or 1 cup mango pulp, 2 cups chilled yogurt (dahi),
half cup milk, sugar 3-4 tbsp (adjust sweetness), pinch of cardamom powder, ice cubes.
This is vegetarian/vegan if using plant yogurt. Ready in 5 minutes. Super easy.

Blend mango pulp with yogurt and milk. Add sugar and cardamom. Blend till smooth and frothy.
Pour over ice in tall glasses. Garnish with a pinch of saffron or pistachio if you want to be fancy.
