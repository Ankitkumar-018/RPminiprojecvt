It was the best of times, it was the worst of times, it was the age of wisdom, it was the age of foolishness,
it was the epoch of belief, it was the epoch of incredulity, it was the season of Light, it was the season of Darkness,
it was the spring of hope, it was the winter of despair, we had everything before us, we had nothing before us,
we were all going direct to Heaven, we were all going direct the other way.

There is nothing either good or bad but thinking makes it so. The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page.
Knowledge is power and power comes from learning. Learning never exhausts the mind; learning only grows the mind stronger.

To be or not to be that is the question. Whether tis nobler in the mind to suffer the slings and arrows of outrageous fortune
or to take arms against a sea of troubles and by opposing end them. To die to sleep no more and by a sleep to say we end
the heartache and the thousand natural shocks that flesh is heir to.

The more that you read, the more things you will know. The more that you learn, the more places you will go.
Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body. A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies.
The man who does not read has no advantage over the man who cannot read.

Time is the wisest counselor of all. Time flies over us but leaves its shadow behind.
Lost time is never found again. The two most powerful warriors are patience and time.
Time is what we want most but what we use worst.

All great achievements require time. Time and tide wait for no man.
In the end it is not the years in your life that count it is the life in your years.
Life is what happens when you are busy making other plans.
